The Curse of the Ember Spire

The sound of a snapping branch is drowned out by your war cry as you burst from the dark thickets...

You cast aside the safety of the shadows and throw yourself into the firelight. The damp air whistles past your ears as you charge, your boots splashing through shallow puddles. For a heartbeat, time seems to snag—you see the grease on their chins, the rust on their blades, and the sheer, paralyzed terror in their yellow eyes. You aren't just a traveler anymore; you are a storm of steel breaking upon their camp.

The chatter turns to high-pitched shrieks of alarm. One goblin trips over a log in his haste to stand, while another frantically kicks embers toward you to blind your advance. Their leader—a scarred brute with a stolen helm—snarls a guttural command, baring jagged teeth. They are cornered, desperate, and reaching for their jagged scimitars. The smell of woodsmoke is replaced by the sharp tang of adrenaline.

The first blow is yours: make it count!
